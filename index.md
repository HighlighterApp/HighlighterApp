## Why Highlighter

- **Built for speed:** mark key moments while recording so your best clips are already found.
- **Built for creators:** turn highlights into montage exports without a heavy editing workflow.
- **Built for privacy:** core recording and processing are designed to stay on your device.

## Core flow

1. Record your session with camera + microphone.
2. Tap to mark highlights in real time.
3. Export a montage video and save or share it.

## Product principles

- **Bold UX:** quick actions, clear controls, minimal friction.
- **Local-first processing:** no required account for core features.
- **Readable outputs:** clean edits that are easy to share instantly.
