**Effective date:** April 5, 2026

## Summary
Highlighter is designed to work locally on your device. It uses your camera and microphone to let you record sessions, mark highlights, and create montage videos. The app does not require an account and is designed not to send your recorded content to the developer's servers for app functionality.

## 1. Scope
This Privacy Policy applies to the Highlighter mobile application.

## 2. What the app accesses
Highlighter may access the following device features and data to provide its functionality:

- **Camera** to show a live preview and record video sessions
- **Microphone** to record audio with video sessions
- **Photos / media library / device storage** to save exported montage videos and access files created by the app
- **Optional connected-device or remote trigger interactions** on supported platforms to trigger highlight actions

## 3. How the app uses this information
Highlighter uses these permissions and features to:

- record a session video
- store highlight timestamps or ranges
- create a montage video on device
- save the exported montage to your gallery or photo library
- share the exported file when you choose to do so

## 4. Local processing
Highlighter is designed so that recording, highlight processing, and montage creation happen locally on your device. The app does not require an account or sign-in for its main features.

## 5. Data sharing and sale
The app is designed not to sell your personal information and not to share your recorded videos or highlights with the developer for app functionality. If you choose to use your device's built-in sharing features, the file will be shared only with the destination you select.

## 6. Data retention
Highlighter may create temporary files while recording or exporting. These temporary files are intended to support app functionality and may be deleted by the app during cleanup. Final exported videos remain on your device until you remove them from your gallery, photo library, or storage.

## 7. Third-party services and platform providers
Highlighter relies on platform features provided by Apple, Google, and the operating system to access the camera, microphone, media library, and sharing features. Those platform providers may handle data according to their own privacy policies.

## 8. Children's privacy
Highlighter is not directed to children under 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

## 9. Security
The app is designed to keep recorded content on your device unless you choose to save or share it through platform features. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 10. Changes to this policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions should be published at the same public URL used for the app listing.

## 11. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the support contact listed in the app store listing for Highlighter.
