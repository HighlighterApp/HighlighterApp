Highlighter:edit (“Highlighter:edit,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 1. Overview

Highlighter:edit is designed to help users capture, edit, and save highlight moments from video recordings. Our goal is to keep your experience simple and privacy-conscious.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide

We may collect information you choose to provide directly, such as:

- Support requests or feedback you send to us
- Contact information you provide when contacting support

### B. Content and Media on Your Device

To provide core app functionality, Highlighter:edit may access:

- Camera
- Microphone
- Photos or media library
- Local files or device storage

This access is used only to enable recording, editing, saving, exporting, and sharing your content from within the app.

### C. App Usage and Device Information

We may collect limited technical information such as:

- App version
- Device type
- Operating system version
- Crash or diagnostic data

This information is used to maintain app performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide and operate the app’s features
- Save, export, and share edited content
- Respond to support inquiries
- Diagnose bugs and improve app performance
- Maintain security and prevent misuse

## 4. Local Processing

Highlighter:edit is designed so that core video capture and editing functions run locally on your device. Your videos and highlight edits are primarily processed on-device rather than sent to our servers for app functionality.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in limited circumstances:

- With service providers that help us operate or support the app, if applicable
- When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of users, the app, or others
